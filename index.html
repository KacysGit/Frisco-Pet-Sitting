About Me

Hi, I'm Kacy Metcalf! With over 11 years of experience caring for pets, including my black lab Jasmine, I am a devoted pet lover and once-certified veterinary assistant. After completing a 5-year veterinary science program and gaining 350+ hours of hands-on experience at Allen Veterinary Hospital, I offer trusted and professional pet care.

I serve Collin and Denton counties in TX, specifically the GrayHawk neighborhood off Eldorado in Frisco. I provide personalized pet care services. Whether it’s my calm senior dog, Jasmine, or my energetic pup Wallie, I’ve cared for all kinds of furry friends!

Services

  • Experienced with small, medium, and large dogs
  • Personalized care tailored to your pet's needs
