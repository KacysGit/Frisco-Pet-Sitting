About Me

Hi, I'm Kacy Metcalf! With over 11 years of experience caring for pets, including my black lab Jasmine, I am a devoted pet lover and certified veterinary assistant. After completing a 5-year veterinary science program and gaining 350+ hours of hands-on experience at Allen Veterinary Hospital, I offer trusted and professional pet care.

I serve the Frisco, TX area, specifically the GrayHawk neighborhood off Eldorado, providing personalized pet care services. Whether it’s my calm senior dog, Jasmine, or my energetic pup Wallie, I’ve cared for all kinds of furry friends!
